Dog Boarding at White Bridge Boarding Kennels

White Bridge Boarding Kennels offers you a home from home for your dog. We have 3½ acres where we can accommodate 42 dogs at a time within the 5 paddocks. Each dog kennel has lighting and thermostatically controlled heating within the bed area and a separate run.

Our approach

Lots of time, love and attention is given to our boarders to make sure their stay is as happy as we can possibly make it. We aim to exercise our dogs several times throughout the day.

Opening hours

We are open all year MONDAY – SUNDAY. All Bank Holidays, including Christmas and New Year.

Drop-off and collection times

Morning drop off and collection 0800 - 1000

Afternoon drop off and collection 1600 - 1700

Alternative times can be arranged but will incur an additional £5.00.

Christmas Eve and New Year's Eve - between 8am and Midday.
Christmas Day, Boxing Day and New Year's Day - no drop off or collection.

Dog food

We supply dog food but if you prefer to provide your own food that's ok too!

Dog's bedding, toys and blankets

Your dog's favourite toy, bedding or blanket are welcome to help them feel at home.
