Day Creche at White Bridge Dog Boarding Kennels

White Bridge creche days for dogs

Our creche days are ideal if you are working and not able to give your dog as much exercise as you would like on certain days or you may want to do your shopping, have a game of golf, go fishing etc. Just drop your dog (or dogs) off for the day and we will keep him (or them) happy, entertained and exercised, ready for you to take home tired and contented.

Opening hours

Creche days for dogs are available MONDAY – SUNDAY. (Excluding Christmas Eve, Christmas Day, Boxing Day, New Year's Eve and New Year's Day)

Drop-off and collection times

Morning drop off and collection 0800 - 1000

Afternoon drop off and collection 1600 - 1700

Alternative times can be arranged but will incur an additional £5.00
