It's playtime at White Bridge Dog Boarding Kennels

Caroline and Keith welcome you to White Bridge Dog Boarding kennels We are situated in North Devon and offer fun and exercise throughout the day for your dogs in secure “‘play pens” set in 3½ acre grounds, whether you drop your dog off for a day creche or your dog is boarding for longer.

At our dog kennels in North Devon it is our aim to keep your dog exercised and happy. We have 5 separate paddocks where dogs can be exercised and socialised in smaller groups dependant on their nature or size. Also dogs can be exercised individually if that is what they prefer.

White Bridge Boading Kennels offers:

  • Large heated kennels with runs
  • Loads of T.L.C.
  • Older residents and puppies welcomed
  • Medication administered (if necessary)
  • Special diets catered for
  • Professional dog clipping/grooming/bathing service available (by arrangement)
  • Inspection warmly invited

White Bridge Boarding Kennels is recommended for dog boarding by:
Local Council Animal Welfare Officer, local vets and a whole host of satisfied customers.
